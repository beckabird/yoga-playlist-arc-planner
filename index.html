How It Works
1
Choose your class — Pick a yoga style, level, and duration below, then hit Generate Arc. Each style has its own energy arc shape (Vinyasa builds in waves, Yin stays low and slow, Power climbs to a steep peak, etc.).
2
Browse your playlist — Songs are matched to each phase's BPM range and energy level. Every song title is a Spotify link — click any song to find it instantly.
3
Make it yours — Drag the dividers on the timeline to resize phases. Click any phase card to edit its name, BPM range, or vibe. Songs auto-update when you adjust sliders. Hit the ↻ button on a card to shuffle for fresh picks.
4
Take it to class — Connect your Spotify account above, then hit Export to Spotify to create a ready-to-play playlist instantly. Or use Copy Full Setlist to grab the list as plain text.
